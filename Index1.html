Coolmuinga Farm

This is the Bonfield farm house located on Coolmuinga Farm in county Clare Knockerra. The Bonfield's first bought this farm over 100 years ago and began with dairy farming mainly, in the last decade the have switched to beef farming as it suits the land and current workers on the farm better.

The cows on the farm are Charolais crossed with Limousins, the Bonfields have picked these as through the years this mix has lead to the most profitable calves to sell in the local mart in Ennis. Another major factor is they tend to be easier calvers and lead to less fatalities which help in making profit and also make the farmers life less stressful.

Information

The clearing has potential to be used as an additional shed for winter housing for calves. It is a large area and would provide substantial area for nuts and silage as well as easy access with farm machinery. The area would also be able to attach to the existing slatted sheds, they have electricty, water and CCTV. This would keep the set up costs to a minimum.

As with all farm machinery, the diggers have the capacity to clear large areas quickly. Large digger are used to clear and reclaim land, and also they go on hire which provides extra income for the farm business. it is essential the people driving the diggers are trained and experienced as they cost in excess of thirty thousand euro.

The meal bin is situated to the front of the slatted sheds. This provides ease of access for feeding. The meal bin holds a large quantity of nuts, five ton and this allows for bulk buying, making it a cost effective way of feeding cattle when they are in for winter housing. There can an issue with rodents and all farms must have controls for vermin and cats.
